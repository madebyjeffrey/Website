SOCR Password Policy

The following password policy is approved by the Chief Technical Officer, July 26, 2013.

In accordance with the ITS Password Standards, SOCR's passwords require the use of three character classes for passwords of eight characters. This means a password can contain a capital letter, a number, and regular letters and be eight characters long.

No dictionary words, or variations on a username are permitted - forward or reverse. It is not recommended to use the same password as you use on any other system.

We use these standards to be as secure as ITS.
